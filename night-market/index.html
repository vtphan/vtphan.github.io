Chợ Đêm: a cultural spectacle of Vietnam

As the sun begins to set, the streets in Saigon transform into a bustling tapestry of life, color, and aromas. From steaming bowls of Phở to crispy Bánh Mì, fresh spring rolls, and other delicacies, Chợ Đêm (Night Market) is a true gastronomic adventure. It's a love letter to Vietnamese cuisine penned by generations of street food vendors.

Our mission is to recreate this magical experience for the very first time in Memphis, Tennessee. Local music lovers will set the rhythm of the night, performing a range of tunes that complement this unforgettable cultural immersion.

Join us at this exciting cultural event!
