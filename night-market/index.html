Chợ Đêm, or the Night Market, stands as a captivating cultural spectacle of Vietnam. As the sun begins to set, the streets in Saigon transform into a bustling tapestry of life, color, and aromas. From steaming bowls of Pho to crispy Banh Mi, fresh spring rolls, and an array of delicacies, Chợ Đêm is a true gastronomic adventure, a love letter to Vietnamese cuisine penned by the hands of generations of street food vendors.

Our mission is to recreate the magic, allowing attendees to immerse themselves in the Vietnamese culture right here in Memphis. Local music lovers will set the rhythm of the night, performing a range of tunes that complement this unforgettable cultural immersion. Join us at this exciting cultural event.
